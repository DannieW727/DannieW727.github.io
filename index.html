About Me

I am currently pursuing my undergraduate studies at Chongqing University. My academic background is in electrical engineering and intelligent systems, and I have developed strong interests in time-series analysis, machine learning, industrial process modeling, and fault diagnosis.

Through coursework, research projects, and co-op experiences, I have worked on both academic and industrial problems, including few-shot fault diagnosis for industrial robot transmission systems and data-driven prediction for blast furnace processes. I enjoy connecting theory with practical engineering applications and building models that are both effective and interpretable.
